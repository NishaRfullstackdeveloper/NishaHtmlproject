Makeup Artist . Educator


Lovingly called the “Queen of Makeup” across industry and by her fans, Parul has come a long way from being a lawyer to one of the most prominent makeup artists in India. Today, she has around 10 million (& growing) followers across Instagram, Facebook and YouTube. Many years before her parent’s could give her permission, Parul Garg would apply the hottest and the coolest shades of lip color to her lips and nail paint to her tiny nails. ” I would sneak in to my much older cousins’ house next door and go bananas on the lipsticks and compact powders! “. Soon Parul’s exploits with makeup were in high demand from her classmates and friends. She set up a small dressing table and a mirror as her first studio where she would transform her friends and relatives! This passion of making everyone more beautiful led her into the world of professional makeup artistry! Coming straight to the present, Parul (a lawyer by education) stopped practicing in 2005 and instead decided to pursue her interest and passion. She opened her own Makeup studio in Gurgaon. She now runs a full fledged Makeup Academy as well. She has served thousands of brides till date. Parul excels in all kinds of looks and makeup but her primary passion remains Bridal and Occasion makeup.
